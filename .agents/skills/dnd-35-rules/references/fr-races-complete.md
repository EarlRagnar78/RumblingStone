# Forgotten Realms — Races & Subraces Complete (D&D 3.5)

Sources: Races of Faerûn (2003), FRCS (2001), Player's Guide to Faerûn (2004),
Underdark (2003), Serpent Kingdoms (2004)

Format: Racial modifiers / Size / Speed / Favored class / Special abilities / Region

---

## HUMANS OF FAERÛN

All human subraces use standard human stats (+1 feat at 1st, +4 skill points at 1st, +1/level)
but have cultural/regional differences and different favored classes.

| Human Subrace | Origin Regions | Favored Class | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| Calishite | Calimshan, Tethyr | Sorcerer | Olive/dark complexion; tend toward magic bloodlines |
| Chondathan | Heartlands, Dales, Cormyr, Sembia | Rogue | Most common "generic" Faerûnian |
| Chultan | Chult | Barbarian | Dark complexion; tribal traditions |
| Damaran | Damara, Vaasa, Moonsea, Thesk | Warrior | Northern; hardy; Slavic analog |
| Illuskan | Luskan, Icewind Dale, Moonshae Isles | Barbarian | Norse analog; pale, tall |
| Mulan | Thay, Mulhorand, Unther | Wizard | Tan/shaved-head; ancient culture; most are enslaved |
| Rashemi | Rashemen | Fighter | Short, stocky; fierce; female mages, male warriors |
| Shaaran | The Shaar | Barbarian | Dark; nomadic; excellent riders |
| Shou | Kara-Tur (eastern continent) | Fighter | Far eastern; rare in Faerûn proper |
| Tethyrian | Tethyr, Amn, Calimshan | Paladin | Mix of Chondathan/Calishite |
| Turami | Turmish, Lake of Steam | Rogue | Dark; Mediterranean analog |

**Regional Human Traits**: Humans gain 1 regional feat for free at 1st level based on homeland.

## ELVES

### Moon Elf (Teu'Tel'Quessir — the People)
**Most common surface elf subrace in Faerûn**
Ability: +2 DEX, −2 CON
Size: Medium | Speed: 30 ft
Favored Class: Wizard
Automatic languages: Common, Elven
Special (all elves): Immune to sleep; +2 save vs. enchantment; Low-light vision;
Weapon Proficiency (longsword, rapier, longbow, shortbow, composite versions);
+2 Spot/Listen/Search; free Search near secret doors
Region: Evermeet, the forests of the North, scattered

### Sun Elf (Gold Elf, Tel'Quessir)
Ability: +2 INT, −2 CON
Favored Class: Wizard
Notes: Haughty, rare on the surface; most common on Evermeet. Consider themselves
superior to other elves. Prefer arcane magic. Average height slightly taller than moon elf.
Region: Evermeet primarily; scattered nobles in major cities

### Wood Elf (Sylvan Elf, Sy'Tel'Quessir)
Ability: +2 STR, +2 DEX, −2 INT, −2 CHA
Favored Class: Ranger
Speed: 30 ft
Notes: Reclusive forest-dwellers. More primitive. Often found in the High Forest, Cormanthor.
Often confuse with wild elves (slightly different). Some sources merge these.

### Wild Elf (Green Elf, Sy'Tel'Quessir)
Ability: +2 DEX, −2 INT
Favored Class: Sorcerer
Notes: Most primitive; distrust civilization; paint themselves for war. Found in deep old forests.
Region: The High Forest, Misty Forest, Forest of Amtar

### Sea Elf (Alu'Tel'Quessir)
Ability: +2 DEX, −2 INT
Speed: 30 ft land, 40 ft swim
Favored Class: Fighter
Extra: Gills (can breathe water + air); primary weapon Trident or spear
Region: Underwater near coastlines; Sea of Fallen Stars

### Drow (Dark Elf, Ssri'Tel'Quessir)
Ability: +2 DEX, −2 CON, +2 INT, +2 CHA
Favored Class: Wizard (female) or Cleric (male) — gender matters in drow society
Special: Darkvision 120 ft; SR = 11 + character level; Spell Resistance;
Drow Spell-like Abilities (Faerie Fire, Darkness 1/day each; Dancing Lights at will);
Light Blindness (blinded 1 round in bright light, −1 attack/checks in shadowy areas);
Weapon Proficiency (rapier, hand crossbow, short sword)
Level Adjustment: +2
Notes: This is a powerful race requiring DM approval. Female drow are dominant in society.
Male drow are second-class citizens except in Eilistraee and Vhaeraun worship.
Region: Underdark; especially Menzoberranzan

### Avariel (Winged Elf) [non-SRD]
Ability: +2 DEX, −2 CON, +2 WIS
Speed: 30 ft; fly 50 ft (good maneuverability)
Level Adjustment: +2
Notes: Extremely rare. Delicate bones (fragile in combat). Most live in isolated mountain aeries.
Region: High mountain peaks, Aerie of the Snow Eagles near Evermeet

### Lythari (Elven Lycanthropes) [NPC race]
Neutral good elves who are natural werewolves. Not playable in standard campaigns.

## DWARVES

### Shield Dwarf (Gold Dwarf of the North — most common)
Ability: +2 CON, −2 CHA
Size: Medium | Speed: 20 ft (not reduced by armor)
Favored Class: Fighter
Special (all dwarves): Darkvision 60 ft; +2 save vs. poison/spells/spell-like; +4 dodge vs. giants;
+2 Appraise/Craft (stone/metal); Stonecunning (+2 Search underground, notice unusual stonework)
Weapon Familiarity: Dwarven waraxe + urgrosh as martial
Region: Shield dwarves in the North, the Underdark, the East

### Gold Dwarf (Hill Dwarf of the South)
Ability: +2 CON, −2 DEX (this is the actual southern variant; not STR based)
Favored Class: Fighter
Notes: More gregarious than shield dwarves; live in the Great Rift (chasm in the Shaar).
Maintain trade with surface nations. Less isolated.
Region: The Great Rift, Underhome (beneath the Shaar)

### Duergar (Gray Dwarf)
Ability: +2 CON, −4 CHA (and −2 to INT for some treatments)
Favored Class: Fighter
Special: Darkvision 120 ft; Immune to paralysis, phantasms, poison; +2 save vs. spells;
Light Sensitivity (−1 attack in bright light); Enlarge Person + Invisibility (1/day each as spell-like);
+4 Racial bonus to Move Silently and Spot; +2 vs. spells and spell-like abilities
Level Adjustment: +1
Region: Underdark; Gracklstugh
Alignment: Usually LE

### Arctic Dwarf (Icerim Dwarf)
Ability: +2 CON, −2 CHA
Special: Cold Resistance 5; Favored class: Barbarian instead of Fighter
Region: The Great Glacier, Novularond (far north)

## GNOMES

### Rock Gnome (Standard, PHB)
Ability: −2 STR, +2 CON, +2 INT, −2 WIS
Size: Small | Speed: 20 ft
Favored Class: Bard (or Illusionist/Wizard)
Special: Low-light vision; +2 Listen; +1 save vs. illusions; Speak with Animals (burrowing, 1/day);
Dancing Lights, Ghost Sound, Prestidigitation (1/day each); +4 AC vs. giants; +2 Craft/Profession alchemy
Weapon Familiarity: Gnome hooked hammer as martial
Region: Gnomish enclaves in major cities; Lantan; the Western Heartlands

### Forest Gnome
Ability: −4 STR, +2 DEX, +2 CON, +2 INT, +2 WIS, −2 CHA
Size: Small | Speed: 20 ft | Favored Class: Ranger
Special: Low-light vision; +2 Listen; Speak with Animals (any, at will); +2 save vs. spells;
Pass Without Trace at will (only in natural settings); Improved Innate Spells
Notes: Extremely reclusive; rarely seen outside of deep forests. Most isolationist of gnomes.

### Svirfneblin (Deep Gnome)
Ability: −2 STR, +2 DEX, +2 WIS, −4 CHA (and INT is not modified but perception is higher)
Special: Darkvision 120 ft; SR = 11 + character level; +2 save vs. illusions; Innate abilities:
Blindsight 30 ft; Blur 1/day; Blindness/Deafness 1/day; Change Self 1/day;
+2 Hide underground; +4 Hide when still; +2 Craft (stone); Move Silently +2
Level Adjustment: +3
Region: Underdark; Blingdenstone (destroyed); various deep gnome enclaves
Alignment: Usually NG

## HALFLINGS

### Lightfoot Halfling (Most Common, PHB)
Ability: −2 STR, +2 DEX
Size: Small | Speed: 20 ft | Favored Class: Rogue
Special: +1 attack with thrown weapons/slings; +2 Listen/Jump; Fearlessness (+2 saves vs. fear);
Lucky (+1 all saves); +2 Climb/Move Silently

### Strongheart Halfling (Faerûn variant)
Ability: −2 STR, +2 DEX
Favored Class: Fighter (not Rogue)
Special: **Bonus feat at 1st level** (replaces the +1 saves of lightfoot);
+2 Listen; Fearlessness; Lucky
Region: Luiren, the Heartlands

### Ghostwise Halfling (Faerûn-exclusive)
Ability: −2 STR, +2 DEX, +2 WIS
Favored Class: Barbarian
Special: Telepathy 100 ft (with chosen target; wordless communication); Cannot speak normally;
Fearlessness; Lucky; +2 Listen
Notes: Most primitive halfling subrace. Cannot be heard if they don't want to be. Live in deep
forest communities. Very rare outside Luiren.

## HALF-RACES

### Half-Elf (Standard PHB)
Ability: None
Special: Immune to sleep; +2 save vs. enchantment; Low-light vision; +1 Diplomacy/Gather Info;
Elven Blood (counts as elf for effects favoring elves); +2 bonus to one skill (varies)
Favored Class: Any
Notes: Extremely common in Aglarond (due to the Yuirwood elves). Common in major cities.

### Half-Orc (Standard PHB)
Ability: +2 STR, −2 INT, −2 CHA
Special: Darkvision 60 ft; Orc Blood (counts as orc for effects)
Favored Class: Barbarian
Notes: Common in the Moonsea region (Thesk, after the Orc Wars). Also in the North.

## PLANETOUCHED RACES

### Aasimar
Ability: +2 WIS, +2 CHA
Special: Darkvision 60 ft; Resist acid/cold/electricity 5; +2 saves vs. poison and disease;
Daylight 1/day; +2 Spot/Listen; Outsider blood
Favored Class: Paladin | Level Adjustment: +1
Notes: Have celestial (angel/archon/eladrin) blood in ancestry. Usually 1–4 generations back.
Common in: Mulhorand (due to deific bloodlines), Waterdeep, large cities generally.

### Tiefling
Ability: +2 DEX, +2 INT, −2 CHA
Special: Darkvision 60 ft; Resist cold/electricity/fire 5; +2 Bluff/Hide;
Darkness 1/day; Outsider blood
Favored Class: Rogue | Level Adjustment: +1
Notes: Fiendish bloodline (usually devil or demon in ancestry). Distrusted everywhere.

### Air Genasi
Ability: +2 STR, +2 DEX, −2 WIS, −2 CHA
Special: Darkvision 60 ft; +1 save vs. air/electricity spells; Breathe Air (never suffocate);
Levitate 1/day; Outsider blood
Favored Class: Fighter | Level Adjustment: +1
Notes: Djinn bloodlines; common in Calimshan (historical djinn rule). Also in Anauroch.

### Earth Genasi
Ability: +2 STR, +2 CON, −2 WIS, −2 CHA
Special: Darkvision 60 ft; +1 save vs. earth/acid spells; Pass Without Trace (stone and soil, 1/day);
Outsider blood; Stone's Endurance (1/day, reduce damage next hit by 10)
Favored Class: Fighter | Level Adjustment: +1

### Fire Genasi
Ability: +2 INT, −2 CHA
Special: Darkvision 60 ft; Resist fire 5; Control Flame (Dancing Lights using fire, at will);
+1 save vs. fire spells; produce flame 1/day
Favored Class: Fighter | Level Adjustment: +1
Notes: Common in Unther and Mulhorand (fire genasi heritage from gods of fire).

### Water Genasi
Ability: +2 CON, −2 CHA
Special: Darkvision 60 ft; Breathe Water; Create Water 1/day;
+1 save vs. water/cold spells; Swim speed 30 ft
Favored Class: Fighter | Level Adjustment: +1

### Chaos Gnome (Chaos Spriggan) [rare]
Exotic; strongly chaotic; not typically playable without DM approval.

## UNIQUE FAERÛNIAN BEINGS

### The Chosen (templates, not races)
Mystra's Chosen: Near-immortal; can use spellfire; +4 save vs. spells; various other powers.
Ao has never created Chosen; it's a deity-specific power.
The Seven Sisters are all Chosen of Mystra (Storm Silverhand, Alustriel, the Simbul, etc.)

### Shades (Netherese Shadow Beings)
Shadow Weave users who survived centuries in the plane of shadow. Treated as outsiders.
Unique stat block; not a PC race. Can be used as major villains/NPCs.
