Lord of Sun and Shadow

Prestige Class (Revised)
"In the breaking dawn, I cast both light and shadow. My path walks between Lathander's radiance and Mask's mysteries, for in duality lies true power."

Description

The Lord of Sun and Shadow represents a rare spiritual awakening among those who channel eldritch power. These warlocks have discovered that true power comes not from darkness alone, but from embracing both the brilliant dawn of Lathander and the mysterious shadows of Mask. Their magic blends divine radiance with shadowy intrigue, creating a unique fusion that allows them to walk between worlds.

Most warlocks are drawn to darker powers, making the Lord of Sun and Shadow an anomaly. Their journey begins when they experience a transformative vision—often during dawn or dusk—where both Lathander and Mask reveal complementary truths about the universe. They learn that light defines shadow, and shadow accentuates light; one cannot exist without the other.

These enlightened warlocks channel their eldritch power through this philosophical duality. Their invocations take on aspects of both divine radiance and shadowy stealth. Their presence can be as blinding as the morning sun or as subtle as a thief in the night, depending on the need of the moment.

Requirements

To qualify to become a Lord of Sun and Shadow, a character must fulfill the following criteria:
  • Alignment: Chaotic Good
  • Class: Warlock 5+
  • Skills: Knowledge (arcana) 8 ranks, Knowledge (religion) 8 ranks
  • Invocations: Must know at least one dark invocation
  • Special: Must have performed a special ritual at dawn while wearing or carrying a symbol of Lathander, and another ritual at dusk while wearing or carrying a symbol of Mask. These rituals require spending at least 500 gp in offerings to each deity.

Alignment Change Consequences

Lost Powers: When a warlock changes alignment to Chaotic Good to take this class, they lose access to any Evil-aligned invocations (such as curse of despair, wall of perilous flame, or dark discorporation). They also lose 1 invocation known of their choice.

Gained Powers: They gain resistance to negative energy equal to their Charisma modifier, and can select from an expanded invocation list that includes Good-aligned options when they gain new invocations.

Class Information

Hit Die: d8

Class Skills

The Lord of Sun and Shadow's class skills (and the key ability for each skill) are: Bluff (Cha), Concentration (Con), Craft (Int), Diplomacy (Cha), Disguise (Cha), Hide (Dex), Intimidate (Cha), Jump (Str), Knowledge (arcana) (Int), Knowledge (religion) (Int), Knowledge (the planes) (Int), Knowledge (history) (Int), Move Silently (Dex), Profession (Wis), Sense Motive (Wis), Sleight of Hand (Dex), Spellcraft (Int), Tumble (Dex), Use Magic Device (Cha).

Skill Points at Each Level: 4 + Int modifier

Class Features

Weapon and Armor Proficiency

Lords of Sun and Shadow gain no additional weapon proficiencies, but they do gain proficiency with light armor if they don't already have it.

Light Armor Mastery (Ex)

At 1st level, a Lord of Sun and Shadow can cast the divine spells granted by this class while wearing light armor without incurring the normal arcane spell failure chance. (They already ignore spell failure for invocations in light armor as per their warlock training).

Twilight Blast (Su)

At 1st level, your Lord of Sun and Shadow levels stack with your warlock levels for the purpose of determining your eldritch blast damage. In addition, your class levels stack with your warlock levels for the purpose of determining the value of your damage reduction (though the type remains Cold Iron unless changed by another ability).

Furthermore, your eldritch blast undergoes a fundamental transformation. The blast becomes a swirling fusion of divine radiance and shadow essence. The damage type becomes mixed: half the damage is divine damage (as per flame strike spell), while the other half becomes fire damage. This mixed damage type bypasses most resistances, though creatures with specific divine damage resistances may apply them to the divine portion.

Example: A 7th level Lord of Sun and Shadow (Warlock 5/Lord 2) counts as a 7th-level warlock for damage, dealing 4d6 eldritch blast damage. On a roll of 18 total damage, 9 points would be divine damage and 9 points would be fire damage.

Shadow's Secrets (Su)

At 2nd level, the Lord gains the ability to hear secrets carried on shadow. Once per day per class level, as a standard action, you can gain insight about a creature you can see within 60 feet. Make a Sense Motive check (DC 15 + target's HD). Success reveals one of the following (your choice): the target's current hit points, one saving throw bonus, one special ability, or their general emotional state and primary motivation. This is a divination effect.

Blessing of the Morning Lord (Su)

At 3rd level, a Lord of Sun and Shadow gains resistance to negative energy equal to their class level + their Charisma modifier. Additionally, they gain a +2 sacred bonus on saving throws against death effects, energy drain, and necromancy spells or effects.

Shadow's Guidance (Ex)

At 4th level, a Lord of Sun and Shadow becomes more adept at stealth and deception under Mask's guidance. They gain a +2 competence bonus to Hide, Move Silently, Bluff, and Sleight of Hand checks. These bonuses increase by +1 for every two additional Lord of Sun and Shadow levels beyond 4th (to a maximum of +5 at 10th level).

Enhanced Twilight Blast (Su)

At 5th level, your Twilight Blast becomes more potent. Choose one of the following enhancements when you attain 5th level:

  • Solar Ray: Your eldritch blast dazzles targets for 1 round on a failed Fortitude save (DC 10 + half your class level + Charisma modifier). Against undead, this effect also deals +1d6 additional divine damage.
  • Shadow Veil: Your eldritch blast creates wisps of shadow around the target. On a failed Will save (same DC), the target suffers a -1 penalty to AC and attack rolls for 1 round.
  • Twilight Mark: Your eldritch blast marks the target with twilight energy. For 1 round after being struck, the target takes 1 extra point of damage from any divine or fire damage sources.

Dawn's Revelation (Su)

At 5th level, once per day, a Lord of Sun and Shadow can invoke the revealing light of dawn as a standard action. This creates bright light in a 30-foot radius centered on you for 1 round per class level. Within this light, invisible creatures become visible (as see invisibility), and illusions of 3rd level or lower are automatically dispelled. Creatures attempting Stealth checks take a -10 penalty.

Twilight Invocation (Su)

At 6th level, once per day, when using any warlock invocation that deals damage, you can enhance it with twilight energy. The invocation's damage becomes half divine, half fire (like your Twilight Blast), and the save DC increases by +1. At 9th level, this ability can be used twice per day.

Example: Using eldritch spear enhanced with Twilight Invocation would change its normal eldritch blast damage to half divine/half fire damage with a +1 DC bonus to any applicable saves.

Shroud of Shadows (Su)

At 7th level, three times per day as a swift action, a Lord of Sun and Shadow can wrap themselves in protective shadows for 1 round per class level. This grants concealment (20% miss chance) and a +4 circumstance bonus to Hide checks. The effect ends if you attack or use a targeted invocation.

Solar Flare (Su)

At 8th level, once per day as a full-round action, a Lord of Sun and Shadow can create a burst of brilliant sunlight. This affects a 15-foot radius centered on you. Creatures in the area take 3d6 points of divine damage and 3d6 points of fire damage (Reflex save for half damage, DC 10 + class level + Charisma modifier). Undead creatures take an additional 2d6 divine damage. You are immune to your own Solar Flare.

Shadow's Embrace (Su)

At 9th level, once per day, a Lord of Sun and Shadow can become semi-incorporeal for up to 3 rounds. As a standard action, you gain the incorporeal subtype, allowing you to pass through solid objects and gain a 50% miss chance against nonmagical attacks. You can still cast spells and use invocations while semi-incorporeal, but physical attacks deal only half damage.

Avatar of Twilight (Su)

At 10th level, once per day as a swift action, you can transform into an Avatar of Twilight for 5 rounds. While in this form, you gain the following benefits:

  • Your Twilight Blast damage increases by 1d6
  • You can use Shadow's Secrets as a move action
  • You gain damage reduction 5/evil
  • You gain immunity to blindness and dazzling effects
  • Your movement speed increases by 20 feet
  • You gain a +2 enhancement bonus to your Charisma score

Invocations

At 2nd, 3rd, 5th, 6th, 8th, 9th, and 10th level, you gain new invocations known and an increase in caster level as if you had also gained a level in the warlock class. You do not, however, gain any other benefit a character of that class would have gained.

Spells

Beginning at 2nd level, a Lord of Sun and Shadow gains the ability to cast a small number of divine spells. To cast a spell, you must have a Charisma score of at least 10 + the spell's level. Saving throws against these spells have a DC of 10 + spell level + your Charisma modifier. You cast spells as a favored soul of your Lord of Sun and Shadow level.

Spells Known: You learn spells according to the progression shown in the class table. When you gain access to a new spell level, you automatically learn one spell of that level from the Lord of Sun and Shadow spell list. At subsequent levels, you may learn additional spells as shown on the table.

Playing a Lord of Sun and Shadow

Combat: In battle, you are versatile and adaptive. Use your enhanced eldritch blast to deal consistent damage while utilizing stealth abilities to position yourself advantageously. Your divine magic provides healing and protection for allies, while your shadow abilities offer tactical advantages through concealment and information gathering.

Advancement: Focus on enhancing your Charisma to improve both your invocations and divine spellcasting. Prioritize skills like Hide, Move Silently, Knowledge (Religion), and Spellcraft. Valuable feats include Ability Focus (Eldritch Blast), Extra Invocation, Spell Penetration, and Point Blank Shot.

Resources: Lords of Sun and Shadow are rare, making formal organizations scarce. Temples of Lathander may provide healing and guidance for your divine aspects, while secretive followers of Mask can offer information and training in stealth arts. Balance your allegiances carefully, as extremists from either faith may view your dual devotion with suspicion.

Compatibility with Ring of Chaotic Illumination

The Ring of Chaotic Illumination's Shadow Step ability (60 ft range, every 5 rounds) is more powerful than any teleportation ability in this class. The class focuses on stealth enhancement, information gathering, and mixed damage types rather than teleportation, avoiding overlap. The ring's enhanced vision and wings provide utility the class lacks, making them complementary rather than redundant.

Lore

"I watched her emerge from the shadows at dawn, hands glowing with divine fire. The undead horde withered before her like morning mist before the sun. In that moment, I understood that light and shadow are not opposed—they are complementary forces in the hands of those wise enough to see beyond dualism."
—Eldrin Mostana, High Priest of Lathander

Lords of Sun and Shadow often serve as mediators and diplomats, their understanding of duality making them natural peacemakers. They excel as investigators and information gatherers, using both divine insight and shadowy techniques to uncover truth. Many become specialized hunters of undead and aberrations, their mixed divine and shadow magic proving particularly effective against such creatures.

Legend speaks of the first Lord of Sun and Shadow, a warlock named Miraleth who experienced simultaneous visions of both Lathander and Mask during a solar eclipse. Both deities are said to have appeared to her, not as rivals, but as complementary aspects of a greater cosmic truth. This dual revelation transformed her understanding of magic and morality, leading to the creation of this unique path.
