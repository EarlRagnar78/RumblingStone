**FASE 1 - INIZIATIVA & APERTURA:**
- Belkram: Iniziativa +3, likely acts early
- Actions: Stand from throne, *Unholy Blight* (10ft radius, 8d8 vs Good)
- Yochlol: Teleport flanchi PG (shadow step 6m no AOO)

**FASE 2 - ENGAGEMENT (Round 2-4):**
- Thorik: Charge trono (Riga 03 → Riga 09 = 9m, possible Round 2)
- Tordek: Flank Yochlol 1 usando Pilastro NW cover
- Yochlol: *Web* spell (center room) OR melee tentacles
- Xanathira: Stealth approach (Hidden until Round 3-4 reveal)

**FASE 3 - CLIMAX (Round 5-7):**
- Belkram: Full attack Thorik (Slam +23, 2d6+16 × 2 attacks)
- Elevation advantage: Belkram +1 AC on dais
- Yochlol: Support Belkram, prevent flanking
- Xanathira: Death Attack (Fort CD 19 or paralysis!) on healer/caster

**FASE 4 - RESOLUTION (Round 8+):**
- Focus Fire: Concentrate on Belkram first (CR 14 boss)
- Yochlol: Flee if Belkram < 50% HP (teleport away)
- Xanathira: Fight to death (fanatic)
- Victory: Belkram 0 HP, corona disponibile prendere
