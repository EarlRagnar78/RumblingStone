# 11 — ATLANTE VISIVO PERSONAGGI E VILLAIN: PROMPT AI

## *Prompt AI per i Personaggi Giocanti (PG) e i Nemici Principali (PNG)*

---

## 1. CONTESTO (non generativo)

> **Scopo:** Questo documento fornisce i prompt standardizzati per generare ritratti, concept art e splash art dei protagonisti (The Rumbling Stones) e dei principali antagonisti della campagna.
>
> **Obiettivo:** Mantenere una coerenza visiva ("Visual Consistency") assoluta attraverso tutte le generazioni AI. Usa questi prompt come "base" e aggiungi l'ambiente o l'azione desiderata alla fine del prompt.
>
> **Stile Globale:** Dark fantasy realistico, ritrattistica di alta qualità, ispirazione D&D 3.5 cover art mischiata a realismo cinematografico (es. Weta Workshop concept art).

## 2. PROMPT DEI PERSONAGGI GIOCANTI (I RUMBLING STONES)

### 📸 2.1 — THORIK (Guerriero)
*Il Manager Strategico, il Tank, il Portatore della Corona*

```
[PROMPT IMMAGINE BASE]
A hyper-realistic fantasy portrait of Thorik, a veteran dwarven fighter. 
He is a broad, muscular shield dwarf with a grey beard and battle-scarred, 
weather-beaten skin. He wears heavy silver and steel plate armor with a 
blue glowing gem set in the chest plate, and a dark red cape. 
KEY ITEMS: 
1) He wears the "Corona di Adamantio" on his head — a glowing golden crown 
set with three large gemstones (yellow, purple, and blue) with a brilliant 
divine halo behind it. 
2) He wields "Aegis Fang" — a masterwork returning waraxe made of gleaming 
steel with a heavy double-crescent blade and intricate runic engravings.
His expression is stoic, commanding, and resolute, standing like a king.

[STILE] Realistic cinematic fantasy portrait, Rembrandt lighting, 
gritty texture on the armor, glowing magical accents from the crown.

[ASPECT RATIO] 4:3 (Portrait)
```

## 3. PROMPT DEI VILLAIN PRINCIPALI (PNG)

### 📸 3.1 — AZARR KUL (Alto Wyrmlord della Mano Rossa)
*Il Generale, Il Nemico Pubblico*

```
[PROMPT IMMAGINE BASE]
A hyper-realistic dark fantasy portrait of Azarr Kul, the supreme 
commander of the Red Hand horde. He is a massive Hobgoblin with 
Half-Blue-Dragon traits. His skin is yellowish-goblinoid but streaked 
with electric turquoise scales along his temples and jaw. Two small, 
sharp blue dragon horns sweep back from his forehead. His eyes are 
vertical, reptilian, and amber. 
He wears heavy, battle-worn black plate armor. The chest plate features 
a dark bas-relief of Tiamat (the five-headed dragon). He wields a massive, 
wicked dark-steel greatsword. He does not scream; he looks upon the 
viewer with cold, tactical, military authority.

[STILE] Epic villain portrait, warlord aesthetic, dramatic stormy 
lighting, realistic textures on dragon scales and scratched iron.

[ASPECT RATIO] 4:3 (Portrait)
```

```
[PROMPT IMMAGINE BASE]
```
