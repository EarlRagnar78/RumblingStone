Physical Description

The ring appears as a circle of obsidian pulsing with an inner violet light. Following the bearer's interaction with the Eternal Forge and their rejection of the Lord of Sun and Shadow path to forge their own destiny, the ring has physically changed.

  • Gold Veins: Thin veins of Lathander's gold now run through the black stone.
  • Silver Glyphs: Mask's silver sigils shift and dance along the rim.
  • Violet Core: The warlock's chaotic essence binds them together.

When active, it projects a faint, harmonious humâ€”the sound of order (Forge) and chaos (Warlock) in perfect tension.

History & Awakening

Originally forged during the Era of Torments, the ring was recently re-awakened in the Hall of the Eternal Forge. The bearer, Artemis, refused the easy path of a Prestige Class offered by the gods, choosing instead to rely on his own chaotic nature. Recognizing this resolve, the ring absorbed the essence of the Mithral Column of Unity and the Dual Flames of the Forge, evolving into a true artifact of balance.

Powers

Constant Powers (Always Active)

  • Enhanced Vision: Superior Darkvision 120 ft, See through Magical Darkness (60 ft), Low-light vision.
  • Mental Bastion: +3 Resistance bonus to Saving Throws (Base Ring property).
  • Forged Knowledge: The ring whispers secrets. +8 Competence Bonus to Knowledge (Arcana) and Knowledge (Religion).
  • Aura of Diplomacy: The essence of the Mithral Column grants a +2 Competence Bonus to Diplomacy.

Combat Synergies (Passive)

  • Dual Flame Blessing: Fire Resistance 10.
  • Eldritch Empowerment: Your Eldritch Blast deals an additional +1d6 Fire and +1d6 Cold damage. (Cumulative with base).

Activated Powers

Wings of Shadow (At-Will)

Action: Free action (Summon/Dismiss). Summons ethereal wings of shadow and violet lightning. Grants Fly speed 60 ft (Good) and the Flyby Attack feat while active.

Shadow Step (1/5 Rounds)

Action: Move action. Teleport up to 60 ft between shadows.

Light of Lathander (1/Day)

Action: Swift action. You emanate Daylight in an 18m (60 ft) radius for 10 minutes. Hope: Allies within radius gain +2 Morale Bonus to Attack Rolls for 3 rounds. Scourge: Light-sensitive enemies (e.g., Black Dragons) suffer -2 to Attack/Damage.

Shadow of Mask (1/Day)

Action: Swift action. Casts Greater Invisibility on yourself AND one ally you touch for 5 rounds. Does not require Concentration.

Unity's Gift (1/Day)

Action: Swift action. Grant one ally within 9m (30 ft) an Extra Standard Action to be taken immediately (even if not their turn).

Firestorm Invocation (1/Day)

Action: Standard action. Unleash a 9m (30 ft) radius burst dealing 8d6 Fire damage (Reflex DC 19 half). Allies are immune.

Crisis Activated Powers

Activate only during involuntary transformation (Cerebromorphosis, etc.)

  • Dawn's Defiance: Automatically triggers Break Enchantment and mental immunity.
  • Solar Purge: Deals 1d6 Divine Damage/HD to aberrations in 30 ft when Dawn's Defiance triggers.

Artifact Synergies

Resonates with Crown of Adamantio and Aegis Fang.

1. Crown + Ring (Passive)

If Thorik and Artemis are within 3m (10 ft), both gain +1 Dodge Bonus to AC vs Lawful creatures.

2. Crown + Ring (Active)

  • Planar Vision (1/Day): Artemis can see through Elemental Portals (Swift).
  • Dispel Illusion (1/Day): Artemis creates 9m burst to dispel illusions (Swift).

3. Aegis + Ring (Dark Dawn Strike)

1/Day Combined Attack: If Artemis casts Light of Lathander on an enemy and Thorik attacks it in the same round, Thorik gains +2 Attack and +2d6 Sacred Damage.

4. Divine Trinity (Crown + Aegis + Ring + Gauntlets)

1/Day (5 Rounds): Requires activation by Thorik, Artemis, and Tordek within 9m. Grants:

  • Immunity: Fear and Charm.
  • Defense: +4 Sacred Bonus to Saves, +2 Sacred Deflection to AC.
  • Offense: Damage overcomes "Good" and "Lawful" DR.
  • Aura: Enemies within 18m must save (Will DC 20) or be Shaken.
