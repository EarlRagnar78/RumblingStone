## Races of Faerûn (FRCS + Races of Faerûn)

### Standard Races (PHB rules apply)
Human, Dwarf (Shield/Gold), Elf (Moon/Sun/Wood/Wild/Sea/Drow), Gnome (Rock/Forest/Svirfneblin), Halfling (Lightfoot/Strongheart/Ghostwise), Half-Elf, Half-Orc

### Faerûn-Specific Subraces
**Moon Elf** (most common surface elf): +2 DEX, −2 CON; Favored class: Wizard
**Sun Elf**: +2 INT, −2 CON; Favored class: Wizard; rarer, haughty
**Wild Elf**: +2 DEX, −2 INT; Favored class: Sorcerer; forest-dwelling
**Drow**: +2 DEX, −2 CON, +2 INT, +2 CHA; SR 11+level; light sensitivity; Favored class: Wizard (F) or Cleric (M)

**Shield Dwarf**: STD dwarf stats; Favored class: Fighter
**Gold Dwarf** (Stout): +2 CON, −2 DEX (not STR as common misread)

**Strongheart Halfling**: bon feat at 1st (replaces +1 to saves)
**Ghostwise Halfling**: Telepathy 100 ft (can't speak aloud) — Faerun-exclusive

## The Weave and the Shadow Weave

**The Weave**: The fabric of magic in Faerûn, maintained by Mystra. Spellcasters tap into it.
**Shadow Weave**: Shar's corruption; used by followers. +1 CL to Illusion/Enchantment/Darkness; −2 to Evocation/Transmutation; cannot learn Divination. (See Shadow Weave Magic feat, FRCS)

**Dead Magic Zones**: Areas where the Weave is destroyed. No magic functions.
**Wild Magic Zones**: The Weave is unstable. Roll on wild surge table.

## Notable Locations

**Waterdeep**: The City of Splendors. Pop. ~132,000 (city) to 1.3M (total). Ruled by hidden Lords. Guilds dominate commerce. Castle Waterdeep; Undermountain beneath.

**Baldur's Gate**: Seat of the Flaming Fist mercenary company. Major trade hub. Two dukes + council of four.

**Neverwinter**: The City of Skilled Hands. Famous for craftsmanship. Near Mount Hotenow (volcano).

**Menzoberranzan**: Drow city of 20,000+. Ruled by noble houses; House Baenre at top. Location: Northdark Underdark.

**Myth Drannor**: Ancient elven city; now ruins haunted by fiends. Recently reclaimed partially by elves (post-1369 DR events).

**Undermountain**: Megadungeon beneath Waterdeep; created by Halaster Blackcloak; 23+ known levels.

## The Spellplague Warning (4e retcon — ignore for 3.5 play)
The Spellplague (1385 DR) is a **4th Edition event**. In a 3.5 campaign set in 1372 DR:
- Mystra lives
- The Weave is intact
- Toril and Abeir are separate
- Do NOT incorporate 4e/5e lore unless the player explicitly wants it

## Calendar of Harptos (Faerûn)

12 months of 30 days + 5 intercalary days (6 in leap years every 4 years):
Hammer, Midwinter (festival), Alturiak, Ches, Tarsakh, Greengrass, Mirtul, Kythorn, Flamerule, Shieldmeet (leap), Eleasis, Eleint, Highharvestide, Marpenoth, Uktar, Feast of the Moon, Nightal

**1 tenday = 10 days** (not weeks; no 7-day week in Faerûn)
