Special Qualities

  • Ancestral Spirit (Su): Any dwarf within 60 feet of a dwarf ancestor gains a +2 morale bonus on attack rolls, damage rolls, and saving throws against fear.
  • Blink Out (Su): Three times per day, a dwarf ancestor can become incorporeal for up to 1 minute. Activating this ability is a free action that does not provoke attacks of opportunity. While incorporeal, the ancestor gains a 50% miss chance and can move through objects.
  • Ancestral Strike (Su): Once per day, the dwarf ancestor can imbue its weapon with the power of its ancestors. For 1 round, its weapon deals an additional 4d6 force damage on a successful hit.
  • Stonecunning (Ex): The dwarf ancestor gains a +4 racial bonus on Search checks to notice unusual stonework, such as traps or hidden doors.
  • Stability (Ex): The dwarf ancestor gains a +4 bonus on ability checks made to resist being bull rushed or tripped when standing on the ground.

Tactics

The dwarf ancestor is a formidable guardian, using its Power Attack and Cleave to devastate groups of enemies. It activates Blink Out to avoid powerful attacks or reposition itself. Its Ancestral Strike is reserved for particularly dangerous foes. The ancestor prioritizes protecting dwarven allies, using its Ancestral Spirit aura to bolster them in combat.
