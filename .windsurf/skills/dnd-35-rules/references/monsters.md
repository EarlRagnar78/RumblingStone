## Common Monster Special Abilities

**Darkvision**: See in total darkness up to stated range (B&W).
**Low-light vision**: See 2× as far as humans in dim light.
**Scent**: Detect creatures by smell (30 ft; 60 ft downwind; 15 ft upwind); pinpoints within 5 ft.
**Tremorsense**: Detect vibrations in contact with ground up to N ft.
**Blindsight**: Perceive surroundings without vision up to N ft.

**DR (DR N/type)**: Reduce damage by N unless weapon has bypass type (magic, silver, cold iron, adamantine, alignment, etc.)
**SR (SR N)**: Attacker must beat d20+CL vs. SR to affect with spell.
**Energy Resistance N (type)**: Reduce that energy type damage by N per attack.
**Energy Immunity**: Completely immune to that energy type.
**Fast Healing N**: Regain N HP /rnd automatically (does not work if dead or at 0).
**Regeneration N**: As fast healing but also regrows lost limbs; certain damage (often fire/acid) deals lethal even to this creature.

**Poison**: Two saves required (Fort); initial and secondary effect (usually 1 min apart).
**Disease**: Fort save or contract; incubation period then recurring damage.
**Paralysis**: Fort save or paralyzed for duration.
**Petrification**: Fort save or turned to stone.
**Swallow Whole**: mel attack; grapple check; swallowed take crushing+acid each round; can cut free from inside.

## Templates

**Half-Dragon**: +2 STR, +2 INT/CHA; gain energy immunity; breath weapon; NA +4; LA +3
**Half-Fiend**: +4 STR/CON, +2 INT/WIS/CHA; SR 11+HD; DR 5/magic; Sp abilities; wings; LA +4
**Half-Celestial**: +4 STR/CON, +2 INT/WIS/CHA; SR 11+HD; DR 5/magic; Sp abilities; wings; LA +4
**Vampire**: Undead type; +6 STR, +2 DEX, +4 INT/WIS/CHA; +8 NA; energy drain; blood drain; dominate; mist form; weaknesses (sunlight, running water, stake); LA +8
**Lich**: Undead type; +2 INT/WIS/CHA; touch paralysis; phylactery; CR +2; LA +4
**Skeleton**: Undead; lose all; gain DR 5/bludgeoning; darkvision 60 ft; CR varies
**Zombie**: Undead; lose all; gain DR 5/slashing; single damage type; CR varies

**Adding a template**: Apply stat changes, gain/lose abilities, recalculate CR.

## Expanded Splatbook Monsters (Cannath Vale Setting)

### The Wyrmbones & Red Hand Horde
*Sourced mainly from Draconomicon, Monster Manual III, and IV.*
- **Abishai (White/Black/Green/Blue/Red)** (*MM IV*): Tiamat's draconic/fiendish shock troops. Excellent for the Fane of Tiamat or as elite liege-lords in the Wyrmbones.
- **Dracotaur** (*MM III*) & **Dragonnels** (*Draconomicon*): Scaly draconic cavalry heavily utilized by the Red Hand when wargs are not enough.
- **Hobgoblin Warsinger / Duskblade / Spellscourge** (*MM III / MM IV*): The elite tactical spellcasters acting as officers of the Red Hand horde.

### Bandit Wastes & Ghostlord's Lair
*Sourced mainly from Libris Mortis, Sandstorm, and Heroes of Horror.*
- **Boneclaw** & **Boneyard** (*Libris Mortis*): Huge, hulking monstrosities created by the Ghostlord to guard his territory. Boneclaws have Ex reach and vicious claws.
- **Blaspheme** & **Visage** (*Libris Mortis*): Advanced undead focused on infiltration and sheer terror.
- **Ashen Husk** & **Wastestrider** (*Sandstorm*): Desert-themed undead wandering the arid desolations of the Bandit Wastes.
- **Salt Mummy / Dry Lich Templates** (*Sandstorm*): Alternate advanced templates for the Ghostlord's elite lieutenants.

### Lhespenbog & Ruins of Lhesper
*Sourced mainly from Stormwrack, Underdark, and Heroes of Horror.*
- **Bog Imp** & **Merrow** (Aquatic ogres) (*Stormwrack / MM*): Aquatic ambushers lurking in the drowned ruins.
- **Chuul** & **Tainted Minion** (*Heroes of Horror*): Aberrations corrupted by Regiarix (the black dragon) and the ambient taint of the swamp.

### The North Wall & Underdark Depths
*Sourced mainly from Lords of Madness and Underdark.*
- **Grell & Grell Patriarch** (*Lords of Madness*): Tentacled terrors featured in ancient mines (like Arc 01 La Miniera).
- **Chitine & Choldrith** (*Underdark*): Lolth-sworn spider-humanoid researchers often found in Fungi Towers (e.g., Sonjak's researchers).
- **Fleshwarped Creatures** (*Lords of Madness*): Grotesque, failed drow experiments dumped in the Neverlight Grove.
- **Myconid Sovereign** (*Underdark*): Denizens of the great fungi towers, wielding powerful spores.

### Eternal Forge & Planar Nodes
*Sourced mainly from Monster Manual and Manual of the Planes.*
- **Crystalline Creatures** & **Xorn** (*Underdark / SRD*): Exploring the elemental Earth Node and the Forest of Living Crystal.
- **Mithral Golem** & **Magma Ooze**: Mighty constructs and slimes bridging the Fire and Earth planar nodes.

### Astral & Transitive Planes
*Sourced from Expanded Psionics Handbook and Monster Manual.*
- **Githyanki Knights / Gish**: Astral raiders riding red dragon mounts. They might appear in the Faerûn skies or the Underdark hunting down mind flayer enclaves (like Zalkatar's) or retrieving stolen silver swords.
- **Githzerai Monks**: ascetic psionic monks from Limbo who also hunt illithids, but often clash with their Githyanki cousins.
