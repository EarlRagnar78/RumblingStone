## REGIONAL FEATS (Player's Guide to Faerûn)

Regional feats require a specific birthplace or upbringing background.
One regional feat may be taken for free at 1st level (replacing the Bonus Human Feat for humans,
or an equivalent option as DM allows). They can also be taken as normal feats if requirements met.

### Aglarond
**Luck of Heroes**: +1 luck bonus to all saves; +1 luck bonus to AC. (Req: Aglarond region)
**Simbul's Glare**: Once per day, attempt a gaze attack that deals 1d6/2 levels fire damage (Ref half). (Req: Aglarond, female only)

### Amn
**Silver Palm**: +2 Appraise, +2 Diplomacy when negotiating prices. (Req: Amn)
**Cosmopolitan**: Pick one non-class skill; it becomes a class skill for you. (Req: Amn, Sembia, or major city)

### Calimshan
**Deft Hands**: +2 Sleight of Hand, +2 Open Lock. (Req: Calimshan)
**Smooth Talk**: +2 Bluff, +2 Diplomacy. (Req: Calimshan or Dragon Coast)
**Genie-Touched**: +2 save vs. fire or air effects (choose type based on ancestry). (Req: Calimshan, Genasi blood)

### Chessenta
**Warrior's Valor**: Once per day, make one attack at full BAB as a swift action (even if already attacked). (Req: Chessenta, BAB +3)

### Chult
**Chultan Jungle Fighter**: +2 attack against creatures you're flanking; +1 AC in jungle terrain. (Req: Chult, BAB +1)
**Serpent Blood**: +2 save vs. poison; +2 damage with unarmed attacks when attacking poisonous creatures. (Req: Chult)

### Cormyr
**Cormyrean Conscript**: Proficiency with longbow; +1 attack with longbow. (Req: Cormyr)
**Militia (Cormyr)**: +1 attack with longsword, +2 Intimidate when in uniform. (Req: Cormyr)

### Dalelands
**Forester**: +2 Hide and Move Silently in forests; +1 attack vs. orcs and goblinoids. (Req: Dalelands or Western Heartlands)
**Survivor**: +2 Survival; once per day, treat a failed save vs. a natural hazard as a success. (Req: The North, Dalelands)

### Dragon Coast
**Mercantile Background**: +4 Appraise; start with 2× the normal wealth for your class. (Req: Dragon Coast, Sembia, Amn)

### Great Dale / The North (Druidic)
**Wychlaran Initiate**: Access to Wychlaran magical traditions; +2 Concentration, Spellcraft when casting in Rashemen's spirit lands. (Req: Female Rashemi, must be Wychlaran member)

### Halruaa
**Magical Artisan**: Reduce time and cost to create any one type of magic item by 25%. (Req: Halruaa)
**Spellwise**: +2 all Knowledge (arcana) and Spellcraft checks. (Req: Halruaa or Silverymoon)
**Arcane Preparation**: Once per day, prepare one arcane spell spontaneously (as divine casters). (Req: Halruaa, arcane spellcaster)

### Impiltur
**Militia (Impiltur)**: +1 attack with longsword and light crossbow. (Req: Impiltur)

### Moonshaes
**Ffolk Heritage**: +2 Survival; speak Ffolk dialect; +2 vs. cold-weather hazards. (Req: Moonshaes, Ffolk human)
**Northman Heritage**: +2 Intimidate; +2 damage with greataxe. (Req: Moonshaes, Illuskan human)

### Mulhorand
**Heroic Destiny**: Once per day, reroll any one die roll and take the better result. (Req: Mulhorand, Mulan human)
**Blooded**: +2 Initiative; +2 save vs. fear. (Req: Mulhorand, Rashemen, or Vaasa/Damara)

### Rashemen
**Rashemi Elemental Affinity**: Choose one element (fire, earth, air, water); +2 caster level for that element's spells. (Req: Rashemen)
**Spirit Speaker**: Communicate with Rashemen land spirits; +4 Diplomacy with fey and nature spirits. (Req: Rashemen, Wis 13+)

### Sembia
**Mercantile Background**: See Dragon Coast above.
**Cosmopolitan**: See Amn above.

### The Shaar
**Shaaryan Mounted Combat**: Mounted Combat + +2 Ride; no penalty for attacking with a bow while mounted. (Req: The Shaar, Ride 4 ranks)
**Track (Regional)**: As Ranger class feature; bonus to Survival when tracking. (Req: The Shaar, various frontier regions)

### Thay
**Tattoo Focus**: Choose one school of magic; +1 caster level for spells of that school while tattooed. (Req: Thay)
**Arcane Schooling**: Select one arcane caster class; that class counts as your favored class instead. (Req: Thay, Luskan, Silverymoon, or Halruaa — represents formal arcane education)

### Unther
**Survivor**: See Dalelands above.
**Warrior's Valor**: See Chessenta above. (Cross-regional for Untheric warriors)

### Waterdeep
**Waterdhavian Noble**: +2 Diplomacy; access to noble social circles; +1 Bluff. (Req: Waterdeep, noble family)

### The Western Heartlands
**Militia (various towns)**: +1 attack with longsword; proficiency with longbow. (Req: Specific Heartlands town)
**Forester**: See Dalelands above.

## DIVINE FEATS (Faiths & Pantheons / Complete Divine)

Divine feats require the ability to Turn or Rebuke Undead. They use one Turn/Rebuke attempt
as a free action to activate the effect (swift action per Complete Divine).

### Tier 1 — Low Investment

**Divine Cleansing**: Use Turn/Rebuke to give nearby allies +4 sacred/profane bonus to saves
vs. disease and poison for 1 minute. (Req: Turn or Rebuke Undead)

**Divine Resistance**: Use Turn/Rebuke to give nearby allies resistance 10 to one energy type
for 1 minute. (Req: Turn or Rebuke Undead)

**Divine Shield**: Use Turn/Rebuke to give yourself +4 sacred bonus to AC for 1 minute.
(Req: BAB +1, Turn or Rebuke Undead)

**Divine Might**: Add CHA modifier to weapon damage for 1 minute (free action when attacking).
(Req: STR 13, Power Attack, Turn or Rebuke Undead)

### Tier 2 — Moderate Investment

**Extra Turning**: +4 Turn/Rebuke attempts per day. May be taken multiple times.
(Req: Turn or Rebuke Undead)

**Sacred Vengeance**: Deal +2d6 sacred/profane damage vs. undead on each attack for 1 round.
(Req: Turn or Rebuke Undead)

**Anchoring Aura**: Prevent extraplanar creatures within 30 ft from plane-shifting or teleporting
as long as you have Turn attempts remaining. (Req: CHA 14, Turn or Rebuke Undead)

**Disciple of the Sun**: Evil undead automatically destroyed when Turned (on a Turn result, not
just rebuke). (Req: Good alignment, Cleric or Paladin 5+)

### Tier 3 — High Investment (Complete Divine)

**Empower Turning**: Maximise the number of HD affected by your Turn attempt (spend 2 attempts).
(Req: Turn or Rebuke Undead 6/day)

**Holy Radiance**: Flood the area with divine light; undead take 2d6+CHA damage and must flee.
(Req: Good, CHA 15, Turn Undead 8/day)

## FR-SPECIFIC GENERAL FEATS

### Spellfire Wielder (Extremely Rare)
Req: Born with Spellfire ability (DM discretion)
Effect: Can absorb spell energy (1d6 absorbed per spell level); release as ranged attack (2d6/charge);
maximum 20 charges stored; fire type damage

### Shadow Weave Magic (Shar's Gift)
Req: Worship Shar; INT or CHA 15; nongood alignment
Effect: Use Shadow Weave instead of the Weave.
Benefits: +1 CL for Enchantment, Illusion, and Darkness spells; these spells ignore certain protections
Penalties: −2 CL for Evocation and Transmutation; cannot use Divination above 1st level;
cannot use items keyed to the Weave
Notes: This is the prerequisite for the Shadow Adept prestige class.

### Initiate Feats (Faiths & Pantheons — one per deity)
These are deity-specific bonus feats for qualifying worshippers. See fr-deities-complete.md
for full list per deity. General format:
Req: Worship the deity; appropriate Knowledge (religion) ranks; usually cleric/paladin
Effect: Bonus spell-like ability or combat ability tied to deity portfolio
Examples:
- **Initiate of Mystra**: Detect Magic at will; +2 Spellcraft; arcane insight bonus
- **Initiate of Tempus**: +1 attack when fighting alongside allies; Weapon Focus in deity weapon
- **Initiate of Lathander**: +2 HP per level; free Empower Spell for healing spells

- **Innate Spell** (Player's Guide): Cast one spell 3/day as spell-like ability (no slots); +4 levels

## FIGHTER BONUS FEATS (Standard but heavily used in FR)

Fighter-exclusive bonus feats at every even level. Includes all combat feats marked as fighter bonus.
Key ones for FR-flavor builds:

**Cavalry builds (Purple Dragon Knight)**:
Mounted Combat, Ride-By Attack, Spirited Charge, Trample, Mounted Archery

**Dwarven defensive builds**:
Shield Specialization, Tower Shield Proficiency, Combat Expertise, Improved Shield Bash

Point Blank Shot, Precise Shot, Rapid Shot, Manyshot, Improved Precise Shot

**Zhentarim enforcer builds**:
Intimidating Strike, Power Attack, Cleave, Great Cleave, Sunder, Improved Sunder

## FEAT INTERACTION NOTES (FR-Specific)

**Regional feats and multiclassing**: Regional feats are taken at 1st character level only. A character
who multiclasses can only have ONE regional feat at character creation; they cannot gain more through
later levels.

**Tattoos (Red Wizard / Thay)**: Tattoos occupy body slots like wondrous items (specific for Thay
rules — DM discretion). The Tattoo Focus feat interacts with specialist school selection.

**Shadow Weave and Mystra's Weave**: A character CANNOT use both. Switching from one to the other
requires special divine intervention (losing the Shadow Weave Magic feat and gaining Weave access).
The two are fundamentally incompatible at a metaphysical level.

**Initiate feats and worship requirement**: If a character ceases to worship the deity in question,
they lose access to that Initiate feat's benefits (but do not lose the feat slot — they gain nothing
from it). Changing deity (atonement + new initiation) can replace the feat's deity.
