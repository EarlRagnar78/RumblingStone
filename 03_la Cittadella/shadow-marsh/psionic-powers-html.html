Shadow Marsh Horror - Psionic Powers Guide

Manifester Level: 7th

Power Points: 35

Base Save DC: 10 + power level + Charisma modifier (+3)

Power Manifestation Rules

Important: As per D&D 3.5 rules, manifesting a psionic power follows similar action economy to spellcasting:

  • Standard powers typically require a standard action
  • Powers can be augmented as part of the manifestation action
  • Manifesting a power typically provokes attacks of opportunity
  • Defensive manifestation can be attempted with Concentration check (DC 15 + power level)

Individual Power Details

1. Psionic Daze

Manifestation Time: 1 standard action

  • Save DC: 14 Will
  • Range: Medium (100 ft. + 10 ft./level)
  • Target: One creature up to 6 HD
  • Power Points: 1
  • Actions Required: Standard action to manifest, free action to augment

2. Recall Agony

Manifestation Time: 1 standard action

  • Save DC: 15 Will
  • Range: Medium (100 ft. + 10 ft./level)
  • Target: One creature
  • Power Points: 3
  • Actions Required: Standard action to manifest

3. Brain Lock

Manifestation Time: 1 standard action

  • Save DC: 15 Will
  • Range: Medium (100 ft. + 10 ft./level)
  • Target: One humanoid
  • Power Points: 3
  • Actions Required: Standard action to manifest, free action each round to maintain concentration

4. Forced Share Pain

Manifestation Time: 1 standard action

  • Save DC: 15 Will
  • Range: Close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)
  • Target: One creature
  • Power Points: 3
  • Actions Required: Standard action to manifest, no action required to maintain

5. Touch of Madness

Manifestation Time: 1 standard action

  • Save DC: 15 Will
  • Range: Touch
  • Target: One creature
  • Power Points: 3
  • Actions Required: Standard action to manifest, can be delivered through tentacle attack

Action Economy Tips

Available Actions Per Round:

  • Standard Action: Manifest one power
  • Move Action: Move up to speed
  • Swift Action: Not used by these powers
  • Free Action: Augment power, drop concentration
  • Full-Round Action: Can be used for full attack with tentacles instead of manifesting a power

Tactical Combinations

Round-by-Round Example

Round 1:

  • Standard Action: Manifest Brain Lock on spellcaster
  • Move Action: Position for tentacle attacks

Round 2:

  • Full-Round Action: Full attack with tentacles
  • Free Action: Maintain Brain Lock concentration

Round 3:

  • Standard Action: Manifest Forced Share Pain on grappled target
  • Move Action: Drag grappled target
  • Free Action: Drop Brain Lock concentration
