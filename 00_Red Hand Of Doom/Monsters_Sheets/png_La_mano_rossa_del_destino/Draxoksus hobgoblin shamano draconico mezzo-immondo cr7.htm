Blue Draconic Adapatation (Sp) Ventriloquism as the spell.

Blue Dragon Totem Gain a line of electricity breath weapon and bluff, hide, and spellcraft as class skills.

Breath Weapon (Su) At 4th level, you gain a breath weapon according to you dragon totem type. It will do 2d6 damage of the appropriate type in the specified area of effect. A successful Reflex save DC (10+CL/2+CON) halves the damage. Just like a real dragon, you must wait 1d4 rounds before using it again. Cone-shaped breath weapons extend out to 15 feet. Line-shaped breath weapons are 30 feet long.

Darkvision (Ex) Range 60 ft.; Darkvision is the extraordinary ability to see with no light source at all, out to a range specified for the creature. Darkvision is black and white only (colors cannot be discerned). It does not allow characters to see anything that they could not see otherwise-invisible objects are still invisible, and illusions are still visible as what they seem to be. Likewise, darkvision subjects a creature to gaze attacks normally. The presence of light does not spoil darkvision.

Draconic Adaptation1 At 3rd level, you take on an aspect of your totem dragon. Some adaptations are extraordinary abilities that are always active and others are spell-like that can be activated at will.

Draconic Aura (Su) You can channel the mighty powers of dragon kind to project an aura that grants you and nearby allies a special benefit. Projecting an aura is a swift action and you can only have 1 active at a time. The bonus granted by your aura is 2. Unless otherwise noted, auras are 30-ft. radius centered on you.

Draconic Resolve (Ex) At 4th level, you gain immunity to paralysis and sleep effects. You also become immune to the frightful presence of dragons.

Dragon Totem You must choose a totem dragon from among the true dragon types (black, blue, brass, bronze, copper, gold, green, red, silver, or white). You must choose a dragon whose alignment is within one step of your own. You gain class skills and breath weapon based on your choice.

Outsider Type

Outsider Type Outsiders breathe but do not sleep/eat (though they can if they wish). Outsiders' souls and bodies are one unit so cannot be restored to life by spells that restore souls to a body (requires a different effect such as a Wish or True Resurrection).

Power (Draconic Aura) (Su) Bonus on melee damage rolls equal to +2.

Presence (Draconic Aura) (Su) Bonus on Bluff, Diplomacy, and Intimidate checks equal to +2.

Resistance to Acid (Ex) You may ignore 10 points of Acid damage each time you take Acid damage

Resistance to Cold (Ex) You may ignore 10 points of Cold damage each time you take Cold damage

Resistance to Electricity (Ex) You may ignore 10 points of Electricity damage each time you take Electricity damage

Resistance to Fire (Ex) You may ignore 10 points of Fire damage each time you take Fire damage

Senses (Draconic Aura) (Su) Bonus on Listen and Spot checks as well as on initiative checks equal to +2.

Skill Focus At 2nd level, you gain Skill Focus as a bonus feat. You must apply the feat to one of the three class skills granted by your chosen totem dragon. You gain additional picks at 8th and 16th level.

Smite Good (Su) Once per day the creature can make a normal melee attack to deal extra damage equal to its HD (maximum of +20) against a good foe.

Special Qualities

A half-fiend has all the special qualities of the base creature, plus the following special qualities.

Darkvision out to 60 feet.

Immunity to poison.

Resistance to acid 10, cold 10, electricity 10, and fire 10.

Damage reduction: 5/magic (if HD 11 or less) or 10/magic (if HD 12 or more).

A half-fiend's natural weapons are treated as magic weapons for the purpose of overcoming damage reduction.

Spell resistance equal to creature's HD + 10 (maximum 35).

Speed A half-fiend has bat wings. Unless the base creature has a better fly speed, the creature can fly at the base creature's base land speed (average maneuverability).

Vigor (Draconic Aura) (Su) Fast Healing 2, but only affects characters at or below one-half their full normal hit points.

Weapon and Armor Proficiency A dragon shaman is proficient with all simple weapons, with light and medium armor types, and with shields (except tower shields).
