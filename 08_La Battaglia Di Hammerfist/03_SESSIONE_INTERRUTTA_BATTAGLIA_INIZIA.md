In this session, we find ourselves amidst the looming threat of the Red Hand army as they lay siege to Hammerfist. The tension is palpable as the players rally to defend the stronghold from impending doom.

- **Time of Attack**: The assault begins at dawn, catching the defenders off-guard.
- **Enemies Approaching**: The sound of war drums echo in the distance, signaling the approach of the Red Hand forces.
- **Initial Defense**: The players quickly mobilize to bolster the defenses and prepare for battle.

- The players strategize on how to best prepare for the inevitable clash, utilizing the terrain of Hammerfist to their advantage.
- Notable character actions include heroic stances and rallying cries to inspire fellow defenders.
