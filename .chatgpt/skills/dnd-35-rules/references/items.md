## Item Creation Rules

**Cost**: Base price ÷ 2 in gp (+ ÷ 25 in XP for most items)
**Time**: Base price ÷ 1,000 gp /day (min 1 day)
**Missing spell**: +5 to crafting DC per missing spell; must make Spellcraft check

**CL of Item**: Can create at higher CL than min (more expensive); CL sets save DCs and duration.

## Pricing Formula (Guideline)

| Item Type | Price Formula |
|---|---|
| Spell effect (1 use) | SL × CL × 25 gp (potions/scrolls) |
| Command word (1/day) | SL × CL × 1,800 gp |
| Command word (unlimited) | SL × CL × 2,000 gp |
| Use-activated (constant) | SL × CL × 4,000 gp |
| Single-use (use-activated) | SL × CL × 50 gp |

Modifiers: ×2 if not slot; ÷2 if 3/day or limited charges; ×1.5 for additional similar powers on same item

## Scrolls

**Activation**: Read Magic to identify; Use Magic Device (DC 20+SL) to use another's scroll
**Failure**: Roll d% < SL percentage → wild surge; CL check to avoid mishap
**Arcane vs Divine**: Arcane casters cannot use divine scrolls without Use Magic Device and vice versa

**Pricing**:
- 0-level: 12.5 gp
- 1st: 25 gp
- 2nd: 150 gp
- 3rd: 375 gp
- 4th: 700 gp
- 5th: 1,125 gp
- 6th: 1,650 gp
- 7th: 2,275 gp
- 8th: 3,000 gp
- 9th: 3,825 gp

## Wands

750 charges max (50 charges/wand manufactured). Each charge = one use of the spell.
**Pricing**: SL × CL × 750 gp (for 50 charges)
- Wand of Magic Missile (CL 1): 750 gp
- Wand of Fireball (CL 5): 11,250 gp
- Wand of Lightning Bolt (CL 5): 11,250 gp
- Wand of Cure Light Wounds (CL 1): 750 gp

**Use Magic Device** to activate wands without the class/spell list.

---

## Intelligent Items

Magic items with Int score: treat as NPCs. Ego score determines who controls conflicts.
**Ego** = item's power rating. If Ego > (char_lvl + Will save + misc), item "wins" control checks.
Intelligent items can communicate: empathy, speech, telepathy (progressively more expensive).

## Expanded Splatbook Items (Cannath Vale Setting)

### Draconic & Horde Gear
*Sourced predominantly from Draconomicon and Red Hand of Doom logic.*
- **Dragondoom (Artifact)**: A legendary weapon intrinsically tied to your campaign. Dragondoom represents an existential threat to dragonkind and is a critical centerpiece artifact in the war against Tiamat's horde.
- **Dragonbane Weapons / Dragonfang Weapons**: Magical armaments highly sought after by the defenders of Rethmar and Cannathgate.
- **Bands of the Blood Glass**: Magical bindings useful for restraining draconic beasts.
- **Goggles of Draconic Vision**: Often found on elite Red Hand scouts, granting darkvision and blind-sense comparable to a true dragon.

### Necromancy & Taint
*Sourced mainly from Libris Mortis and Heroes of Horror.*
- **Nightstick** (*Libris Mortis*): A vitally important magic item for clerics facing the Ghostlord's hordes. A Nightstick grants Extra Turning uses, fueling powerful divine feats (like Divine Metamagic) and radiant blasts to decimate undead.
- **Cloak of Turn Resistance**: An essential item worn by the Ghostlord's elite undead lieutenants (like his favored boneclaws or vampires) to avoid being instantly destroyed by clerics of Lathander.
- **Undead Grafts** (*Libris Mortis*): Grotesque supernatural augmentations (e.g., Zombie Arm, Skeletal Hand). Found in the deepest recesses of the Pale Masters' tombs or the Thornwaste.

### Wasteland & Storm Gear
*Sourced mainly from Sandstorm and Stormwrack.*
- **Goggles of Day / Lenses of the Desert** (*Sandstorm*): Essential survival gear protecting against the blinding sands and blinding sunlight of the Bandit Wastes.
- **Wasteland Clothing / Hydration Suit** (*Sandstorm*): Heavy protective clothing utilized by Bedine refugees or desert scouts heading to face the Ghostlord.
- **Stormwalker's Cloak** (*Stormwrack*): Magical protection frequently worn by the Tiri Kitor elves patrolling the edges of the Wyrmbones or Lhespenbog.

### Underdark & Drow Tools
*Sourced mainly from Underdark and Lords of Madness.*
- **Piwafwi (Drow Cloak)**: Equivalent to a Cloak of Elvenkind but far superior in darkness, crafted by Sonjak's forces in the Neverlight Grove.
- **House Insignias**: Found uniformly on Drow and Duergar strike teams.
- **Silithar Blood/Bone Grafts** (*Lords of Madness*): Disgusting, high-tier aberration augmentations. Prime horrific loot resulting from the fleshwarping experiments in the depths below Hammerfist Holds.
- **Crystal Masks** (*Expanded Psionics / LoM*): Alien magical gear worn by Illithid thralls or Zalkatar's elite guards.

### Astral & Githyanki Gear
*Sourced from Expanded Psionics Handbook and Manual of the Planes.*
- **Githyanki Silver Swords**: Priceless +3 greatswords capable of severing an astral traveler's silver cord and striking incorporeal/psionic entities (req: Psionic Focus/feat in XPH). A Githyanki strike force will *always* hunt down any non-gith wielding one of their sacred weapons.
- **Astral Skiffs**: Psionically propelled vessels that might occasionally breach the material plane to hunt Mind Flayers like Zalkatar or parley with Red Dragons of the horde.
