Shadow Marsh Horror / Orrore Palustre d'Ombra

English Description

From the putrid waters rises a nightmarish entity that defies natural law - a writhing mass of darkness roughly nine meters in diameter. Oily black tentacles, each as thick as a dwarf's torso, emerge from its amorphous form, trailing wisps of shadow that seem to devour nearby light. Its surface ripples with impossible geometries that hurt the mind to observe, while the corrupted water beneath it bubbles with unnatural phosphorescence. The air around it carries the stench of stagnant water and decay, mixed with an alien musk that triggers primal fear in mortal beings.

Descrizione Italiana

Dalle acque putride emerge un'entità da incubo che sfida le leggi naturali - una massa contorcente di oscurità di circa nove metri di diametro. Tentacoli neri e oleosi, ciascuno spesso quanto il torso di un nano, emergono dalla sua forma amorfa, lasciando scie di ombre che sembrano divorare la luce circostante. La sua superficie ondeggia con geometrie impossibili che feriscono la mente al solo osservarle, mentre l'acqua corrotta sotto di essa ribolle con una fosforescenza innaturale. L'aria intorno porta con sé il fetore di acqua stagnante e decomposizione, mischiato con un muschio alieno che scatena un terrore primordiale negli esseri mortali.

Quick Statistics (CR 12)

HP: 133 (14d8+70)
AC: 23
Initiative: +6
Speed: 20 ft., swim 30 ft.

Ability Scores

STR: 22 (+6)
DEX: 16 (+3)
CON: 20 (+5)
INT: 14 (+2)
WIS: 14 (+2)
CHA: 16 (+3)

Core Abilities

  • Shadow Blend: Disappear into shadows as move action
  • Energy Drain: 1 negative level per hit (DC 19)
  • Shadow Essence: 1d4 Strength damage (DC 19)
  • Constrict: 2d6+9 plus 1d6 negative energy
  • Improved Grab: Can initiate grapple on hit without AoO

Psionic Powers (ML 7th, 35 PP)

  • Psionic Daze (DC 14): Standard action, stuns target
  • Recall Agony (DC 15): Standard action, 2d6 damage + stun
  • Brain Lock (DC 15): Standard action, paralyzes target
  • Forced Share Pain (DC 15): Standard action, share damage
  • Touch of Madness (DC 15): Standard action, causes confusion

Tactical Overview

Terrain Advantages

  • Uses corrupted pool for concealment (+4 Hide)
  • Utilizes mine pillars for cover
  • Exploits shadows between light sources
  • Can pull victims into contaminated water (DC 15 swim check)

Combat Phases

Phase 1: Ambush

  • Hides using Shadow Blend
  • Waits for party approach
  • Surprise tentacle attacks

Phase 2: Initial Assault

  • Psionic Daze on spellcasters
  • Grapple closest targets
  • Pull victims toward water
  • Brain Lock on resistant casters

Phase 3: Sustained Combat

  • Maintain grapples
  • Apply Forced Share Pain
  • Use Touch of Madness
  • Drag victims underwater
  • Shadow Blend when damaged

Environmental Effects

Corrupted Pool

  • 15 feet deep at center
  • 1d6 acid damage per round of immersion
  • 20% miss chance due to murky water
  • DC 15 Balance check on bottom

Mine Setting

  • Dim light (20% miss chance)
  • Partial cover from debris
  • DC 12 Balance check near pool
  • Enhanced Listen checks (+2) due to echoes

Weaknesses

  • Limited mobility on land
  • Vulnerable to bright light
  • Can be overwhelmed by multiple targets
  • Limited power points require conservation
