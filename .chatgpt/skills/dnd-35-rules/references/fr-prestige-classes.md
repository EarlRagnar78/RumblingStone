# FR — Prestige Classes (D&D 3.5)

Sources: FRCS (2001), Player's Guide to Faerûn (2004), Champions of Valor (2005),
Champions of Ruin (2005), Races of Faerûn (2003), various FR splatbooks.

Format: Requirements → Key features → Notes

---

## FRCS Core Prestige Classes

### Arcane Devotee
Requirements: Knowledge (religion) 4 ranks; ability to cast 2nd-level arcane spells
HD: d4 | BAB: ½ | Saves: Will good
Key features: bon domain spell (arcane version), Sacred Defense +2 per 2 levels, spellcasting +1/level
Spellcasting: +1 arcane CL /lvl (advances existing class)
Notes: Allows arcane casters to serve a deity and gain domain access.

### Arcane Trickster (SRD + FR variant)
Requirements: Sneak Attack +2d6; ability to cast mage hand; arcane caster
HD: d4 | Key features: rng legerdemain, sneak attack advances, spellcasting advances

### Church Inquisitor
Requirements: BAB +4; must worship a lawful deity; Knowledge (religion) 6 ranks
HD: d8 | BAB: Full | Good saves: Fort, Will
Key features: Detect Deception (as zone of truth continuously), Inquisitor's Domain,
Spellcasting +1/level (divine), Pierce Illusion, Find Heretic, Destructive Smite
Notes: Detective/interrogator for lawful religions. Popular in Torm and Tyr churches.

### Divine Champion
Requirements: BAB +7; must have a patron deity; Knowledge (religion) 4 ranks
HD: d10 | BAB: Full | Good save: Fort
Key features: Divine Wrath (Smite 1/day per 2 levels), Lay on Hands (CHA × lvl HP),
Sacred Defense +1 per 2 levels, Martyr's Death (on death, affect area as per spell)
Notes: Holy warrior tied to a deity. Fighter/paladin path.

### Divine Disciple
Requirements: Cleric or Druid; Knowledge (religion) 9 ranks; Spellcraft 4 ranks
HD: d8 | BAB: ¾ | Good saves: Fort, Will
Key features: 1 domain power/level from chosen deity, spellcasting +1/level,
Transcendence (become outsider of appropriate subtype at level 5)
Notes: Clerics who undergo profound transformation. Rare and powerful.

### Divine Seeker
Requirements: Sneak attack +3d6; Knowledge (religion) 4 ranks; Gather Information 8 ranks
HD: d6 | BAB: ¾ | Good saves: Ref, Will
Key features: Sacred Defense, sneak attack +1d6/2 levels, Locate Target (divine Locate Object),
Woodland Stride, spellcasting +1/level (divine)
Notes: Divine scout/spy. Popular with Mask and Waukeen churches.

### Harper Agent (Harper Scout)
Requirements: Bard 1 or Ranger 2; Knowledge (local) 4 ranks; Knowledge (geography) 2 ranks;
Must be sponsored by a Harper member
HD: d6 | BAB: ¾ | Good saves: Ref, Will
Key features: Bard Song (gains bardic music uses even if not bard), Arcane Spellcasting (1st-3rd
level bard spells), Woodland Stride, Nature's Speech (Speak with Animals 1/day),
Uncanny Dodge, Harper Knowledge (as Bardic Knowledge for local lore)
Alignment: Any good or neutral
Notes: The Harpers' agents. Works well for charismatic ranger/bard hybrid builds.

### Hierophant (SRD)
Requirements: Cleric 7+ or Druid 7+; Knowledge (religion) 15 ranks
HD: d8 | Key features: Special abilities replacing spell slots (Blast Infidel, Summon Planar Ally,
Faith Healing, Mastery of Energy, Power of Nature)

### Purple Dragon Knight
Requirements: BAB +4; Diplomacy 4 ranks; must be Cormyrean military (or equivalent)
HD: d10 | BAB: Full | Good save: Fort
Key features: Rallying Cry (Inspire Courage as bard), Inspire Courage/Heroics/Greatness (later),
Aura of Courage, Purple Dragon Whirlwind (extra attack when killing blow)
Alignment: LG, LN, NG
Notes: Cormyrean military prestige class. Focuses on battlefield leadership.

### Red Wizard of Thay
Requirements: Wizard 5; Region (Thay) or must join Thay; Spellcraft 7 ranks; Wizard specialist;
cannot be barred from using tattoos; must shave head
HD: d4 | BAB: ½ | Good save: Will
Key features: Specialist Defense (specialist bon to saves), Spell Power (+1 CL per 2 levels),
bon Feat (item creation/metamagic), Scribe Tattoo (magical tattoos as magic items),
Enhanced Specialization, Wizard Spell Power (bon spell slot per specialist level)
Alignment: Any nongood
Notes: Most powerful arcane caster PrC in FR. req: significant backstory investment.
Red Wizards trade magic items via enclaves throughout Faerûn — a major economic power.

### Shadow Adept
Requirements: Any metamagic feat; Knowledge (arcana) 8 ranks; ability to cast 4th-level spells
HD: d4 | BAB: ½ | Good save: Will
Key features: Shadow Weave Magic (using Shar's Shadow Weave instead of Mystra's), Spellcasting +1/level,
Tendrils of Darkness (free obscuring mist), Shadow Double (mirror image 1/day), Spell Power +1
Benefits: +1 CL for Enchantment, Illusion, and Darkness spells
Penalties: −2 CL for Divination; −2 CL for Evocation and Transmutation;
cannot use Divination spells from the Weave at all
Alignment: Usually NE or LE (Shar worshippers)
Notes: Shadow Weave users cannot learn Divination beyond 1st level. This is a significant trade-off.

### Spellfire Channeler
Requirements: Spellfire power (extremely rare — usually born with it), Concentration 8 ranks
Notes: Spellfire is the ability to absorb magical energy and release it as destructive fire.
Extremely rare. Most famous wielder: Shandril Shessair.

## Champions of Valor / Valor-Aligned PrCs

### Acolyte of the Skin (evil)
Requirements: Chaotic evil; Concentration 8 ranks; ability to cast 3rd-level arcane spells
Key features: Demon grafted to skin; gains natural armor, claws, and eventually fiendish traits.

### Bladesinger (Elven)
Requirements: Elf or half-elf; Weapon Focus (any sword); Spellcraft 8 ranks; arcane spellcaster
HD: d8 | BAB: ¾ | Good saves: Ref, Will
Key features: Bladesong Style (when in style: +2 AC, +10 speed, arcane spell failure reduced 15%),
Spell Power +1/2 levels, spellcasting +1/level, Extra Bladesong Style uses
Notes: Elven warrior-mage tradition. Common among Tel'Quessir.

### Fist of Eilistraee
Requirements: Good drow; Eilistraee worship; BAB +5; bardic music or Turn Undead
Key features: Song of the Eilistraee (expanded bardic music), Moonfire (radiant damage to evil),
Divine Spellcasting (1st–4th level cleric spells), Moonblade training

### Holy Liberator
Requirements: Good alignment; BAB +4; lay on hands (paladin feature)
Key features: Inspire Freedom (counter fear + charm), Smite Evil, spellcasting (1st–3rd level)

### Initiate of Eilistraee / Ilmater / etc.
Many specific deity initiate PrCs exist in various books. General format:
Requirements: Worshipping that deity; Knowledge (religion) 6+ ranks; various
Key features: Access to extra domain spells, special divine abilities tied to deity's portfolio

### Knight of the Weave (Champion of Mystra)
Requirements: Arcane spellcaster; Knowledge (arcana) 10 ranks; Good alignment
Key features: Weave Sense, spellcasting +1/level, antimagic protection, Weave Channeling

## Races of Faerûn PrCs

### Demonbinder
Requirements: Chaotic evil; Knowledge (the planes) 6 ranks; ability to cast 2nd-level arcane
Key features: Bind Demon (compel demons without using spell slots), Demonic Familiar, Infernal Resilience

### Drow Judicator (Evil)
Requirements: Drow; BAB +6; worship Lolth
Key features: Spider Climb, Poison use +, special drow combat styles

### Gnome Artificer
Requirements: Gnome; Craft (any) 8 ranks; Craft Wondrous Item feat
Key features: Create Minor Artifacts, Imbue Item (create magic items as sorcerer ignoring class requirements)

### Ruathar (Elven Friend)
Requirements: Non-elf; befriended an elven noble house; Knowledge (nature) 4 ranks
Key features: Elven weapon training, low-light vision, Elven Blood (treated as elf for effects)

### Shield Dwarf Defender
Requirements: Shield dwarf; Fighter 4; Toughness feat
Key features: Improved DR, Dwarven Battleskill (+2 attack vs. giants and orcs)

## Unapproachable East PrCs

### Hathran (Rashemi Witch)
Requirements: Female human; Rashemen origin; ability to cast 3rd-level arcane and 1st-level divine;
must be a member of the Wychlaran (Hathran society)
HD: d6 | BAB: ½ | Good saves: Fort, Ref, Will (unusual)
Key features: Cohort of the Land (spirit companion), Circle (join spellcasting circles with other
Hathrans for vastly increased power), Special Arcane Ability (bon power /lvl),
Spellcasting +1/level (both arcane and divine)
Notes: Unique dual-progression caster (advances BOTH wizard and cleric caster levels simultaneously).
Extremely powerful for gestalt-style builds. Must remain in Rashemen or powers are weakened.

### Runescarred Berserker
Requirements: Rashemi barbarian; BAB +6; 5 runes (magical tattoos) already scarred
Key features: Runic Power (each rune grants a Sp ability, 1/day), Rage enhancement,
Improved Uncanny Dodge

### Taer (Rashemi Warrior)
Requirements: Rashemen male; BAB +5; Cannot use arcane magic
Key features: Taer's Rage (enhanced barbarian rage), Two-weapon style training

### Mage Hunter (Theskian)
Requirements: BAB +4; Spellcraft 6 ranks (to identify spells to counter); no arcane spellcasting
Key features: Dispel (as dispel magic 1/day /lvl), SR (growing from 10 to 20+)

## Shining South PrCs

### Forest Master
Requirements: Druid 5 or Ranger 5; Knowledge (nature) 8 ranks; Halruaan or Chultan origin
Key features: Wild Shape advances, Summon Nature's Ally +2 effective druid level

### Ghost-Faced Killer (Assassin variant)
Requirements: Chaotic or evil; Hide 8 ranks; Move Silently 8 ranks; sneak attack +4d6
Key features: Frightful attack (target must save or become shaken), Death Attack, Sneak Attack

### Morninglord of Lathander
Requirements: Cleric of Lathander; Knowledge (religion) 8 ranks; BAB +4
Key features: Sunbolt (rng divine attack), Restoration 1/day, Undead Revulsion (turn bon)

### Wavemistress / Sailor of Umberlee
Requirements: Worship Umberlee; BAB +4; Swim 6 ranks
Key features: Water Breathing, Stormcall (call a storm 1/day), Drown (grapple → drowning)

## Other Notable PrCs

### Shou Disciple (Kara-Tur cultural link)
req:: Far East / Shou origin; Improved Unarmed Strike; multiple martial feats
Key features: Ki pool (as monk), specific eastern martial art style

### Dweomerkeeper (divine magic specialist)
req:: Knowledge (arcana) 10 ranks; cleric 5; ability to cast 4th-level divine
Key features: Spontaneous Domain Spells, Alter Spell (apply one metamagic free /day)

### Loremaster (SRD, but common in FR)
Requirements: Knowledge (any two) 10 ranks; ability to cast 7th-level spells
Key features: Secrets (one bon feat or ability /lvl), Lore (combined bardic+wizard knowledge check)

### Archmage (SRD, used in FR)
Requirements: Arcane caster; ability to cast 7th-level arcane; 4 metamagic feats; Spellcraft 15
Key features: High Arcana abilities (Arcane Fire, Arcane Reach, Mastery of Counterspelling,
Mastery of Elements, Mastery of Shaping, Spell Power, Sp Ability)
Notes: Elminster-level PrC for NPC archmages.
