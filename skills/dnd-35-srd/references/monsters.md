## Stat Block Format (Standard)

```
[Monster Name]
Size/Type: [Size] [Type] [(Subtype)]
Hit Dice: [N]d[HD]+[CON bonus] ([avg HP] hp)
Initiative: +[mod]
Speed: [N] ft. (N squares)[, fly N ft. (maneuverability)]
Armor Class: [N] ([breakdown]), touch [N], flat-footed [N]
Base Attack/Grapple: +[BAB]/+[grapple]
Attack: [weapon] +[mod] melee/ranged ([damage]/[crit])
Full Attack: [all attacks]
Space/Reach: [N] ft./[N] ft.
Special Attacks: [list]
Special Qualities: [list]
Saves: Fort +[N], Ref +[N], Will +[N]
Abilities: Str [N], Dex [N], Con [N], Int [N], Wis [N], Cha [N]
Skills: [skill] +[N], ...
Feats: [list]
Environment: [terrain]
Organization: [solitary/pair/group (N–N)]
Challenge Rating: [N]
Treasure: [type]
Alignment: [alignment]
Advancement: [N–N HD (Size)]
Level Adjustment: [N or —]
```

## Common Monster Special Abilities

**Darkvision**: See in total darkness up to stated range (B&W).
**Low-light vision**: See 2× as far as humans in dim light.
**Scent**: Detect creatures by smell (30 ft; 60 ft downwind; 15 ft upwind); pinpoints within 5 ft.
**Tremorsense**: Detect vibrations in contact with ground up to N ft.
**Blindsight**: Perceive surroundings without vision up to N ft.

**Damage Reduction (DR N/type)**: Reduce damage by N unless weapon has bypass type (magic, silver, cold iron, adamantine, alignment, etc.)
**Spell Resistance (SR N)**: Attacker must beat d20+caster level vs. SR to affect with spell.
**Energy Resistance N (type)**: Reduce that energy type damage by N per attack.
**Energy Immunity**: Completely immune to that energy type.
**Fast Healing N**: Regain N HP per round automatically (does not work if dead or at 0).
**Regeneration N**: As fast healing but also regrows lost limbs; certain damage (often fire/acid) deals lethal even to this creature.

**Poison**: Two saves required (Fort); initial and secondary effect (usually 1 min apart).
**Disease**: Fort save or contract; incubation period then recurring damage.
**Paralysis**: Fort save or paralyzed for duration.
**Petrification**: Fort save or turned to stone.
**Swallow Whole**: Melee attack; grapple check; swallowed take crushing+acid each round; can cut free from inside.

## Improving Monsters (Advancement)

Source: https://www.d20srd.org/srd/improvingMonsters.htm
Full workflow + decision framework: see the `npc-villain-boosting` skill.

**Adding Hit Dice** (stay within the stat block's `Advancement:` range):
each HD adds hp (type die + Con), BAB/saves per type progression, skill
points, a feat at every 3rd total HD, +1 ability at every 4th total HD.

**CR increase from added HD** (Table: Improved Monster CR Increase):

| Original type | CR +1 per |
|---|---|
| Aberration, construct, elemental, fey, giant, humanoid, ooze, plant, undead, vermin | 4 HD |
| Animal, magical beast, monstrous humanoid | 3 HD |
| Dragon, outsider | 2 HD |

**Changes to Statistics by Size** (when advancement crosses a size bracket):

| Old → New | Str | Dex | Con | Natural armor |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Tiny → Small | +4 | −2 | — | — |
| Small → Medium | +4 | −2 | +2 | — |
| Medium → Large | +8 | −2 | +4 | +2 |
| Large → Huge | +8 | −2 | +4 | +3 |
| Huge → Gargantuan | +8 | — | +4 | +4 |
| Gargantuan → Colossal | +8 | — | +4 | +5 |

Repeat the adjustment for each size category gained. Also recompute size
mods to attack/AC (M +0, L −1, H −2, G −4, C −8), grapple (L +4, H +8,
G +12, C +16), space/reach; step damage dice up one size per category
(1d2→1d3→1d4→1d6→1d8→2d6→3d6; 1d10→2d8→3d8).

**Other CR modifiers** (same SRD table — these are RAW, not guidance):

| Modifier | CR |
|---|---|
| Size increased to Large or larger | +1 |
| Ability scores based on elite array (not if advanced by class levels) | +1 |
| New special attacks/qualities that significantly improve combat | +2 |
| New special attacks/qualities that improve combat in a minor way | +1 |
| Template added | + template CR modifier |

Do **not** stack the added-HD CR increase with a class-level CR increase.

**Adding class levels**: associated class (amplifies the monster's
strengths — e.g. fighter/barbarian on a combat brute; a casting class that
stacks with innate casting) = CR +1/level; nonassociated = CR +1 per
2 levels until the nonassociated class levels equal the original racial HD,
then +1/level; NPC classes always nonassociated. Give PC-classed monsters
the elite array (15/14/13/12/10/8), NPC-classed the nonelite array
(13/12/11/10/9/8); unclassed monsters assume 11/11/11/10/10/10 + racial.
Humanoids with 1 racial HD swap it for the first class level (then: PC
classes CR = level, NPC classes CR = level − 1).

## Templates

**Half-Dragon**: +8 STR, +2 CON/INT/CHA; energy immunity; breath weapon 1/day; NA +4; CR +2; LA +3
**Half-Fiend**: +4 STR/DEX/INT, +2 CON/CHA; SR, DR 5/magic; SLAs by HD; wings; CR +1 (HD ≤4), +2 (HD 5–10), +3 (HD 11+); LA +4
**Half-Celestial**: +4 STR/CON/WIS/CHA, +2 DEX/INT; SR, DR 5/magic; SLAs by HD; wings; CR +1 (HD ≤5), +2 (HD 6–10), +3 (HD 11+); LA +4
**Celestial / Fiendish**: darkvision 60 ft; resist acid/cold/electricity (celestial) or cold/fire (fiendish) 5 (HD 1–3/4–7) or 10 (HD 8+); DR — (HD 1–3), 5/magic (HD 4–11), 10/magic (HD 12+); SR HD+5 (max 25); smite 1/day (+HD dmg, max +20); CR +0 (HD ≤3), +1 (HD 4–7), +2 (HD 8+); LA +2
**Vampire**: Undead type; +6 STR, +4 DEX, +2 INT/WIS, +4 CHA; +6 NA; energy drain; blood drain; dominate; gaseous form; fast healing 5; DR 10/silver+magic; weaknesses (sunlight, running water, stake); CR +2; LA +8
**Lich**: Undead type; +2 INT/WIS/CHA; touch attack + paralysis; fear aura; phylactery; DR 15/bludgeoning+magic; CR +2; LA +4
**Ghost**: Undead (incorporeal); manifestation; +4 CHA; frightful moan/malevolence etc. (pick powers); CR +2; LA +5
**Skeleton**: Undead; recalculated from HD; DR 5/bludgeoning; darkvision 60 ft; immune to cold
**Zombie**: Undead; HD doubled; DR 5/slashing; single move OR attack per round

**Adding a template**: apply stat changes, gain/lose abilities, recalculate
CR, then verify ability lines against the SRD template page (the one-liners
above are load-bearing summaries, not full rules text).

## Expanded Splatbook Monsters (Cannath Vale Setting)

### The Wyrmbones & Red Hand Horde
*Sourced mainly from Draconomicon, Monster Manual III, and IV.*
- **Abishai (White/Black/Green/Blue/Red)** (*MM IV*): Tiamat's draconic/fiendish shock troops. Excellent for the Fane of Tiamat or as elite liege-lords in the Wyrmbones.
- **Dracotaur** (*MM III*) & **Dragonnels** (*Draconomicon*): Scaly draconic cavalry heavily utilized by the Red Hand when wargs are not enough.
- **Hobgoblin Warsinger / Duskblade / Spellscourge** (*MM III / MM IV*): The elite tactical spellcasters acting as officers of the Red Hand horde.

### Bandit Wastes & Ghostlord's Lair
*Sourced mainly from Libris Mortis, Sandstorm, and Heroes of Horror.*
- **Boneclaw** & **Boneyard** (*Libris Mortis*): Huge, hulking monstrosities created by the Ghostlord to guard his territory. Boneclaws have extraordinary reach and vicious claws.
- **Blaspheme** & **Visage** (*Libris Mortis*): Advanced undead focused on infiltration and sheer terror.
- **Ashen Husk** & **Wastestrider** (*Sandstorm*): Desert-themed undead wandering the arid desolations of the Bandit Wastes.
- **Salt Mummy / Dry Lich Templates** (*Sandstorm*): Alternate advanced templates for the Ghostlord's elite lieutenants.

### Lhespenbog & Ruins of Lhesper
*Sourced mainly from Stormwrack, Underdark, and Heroes of Horror.*
- **Bog Imp** & **Merrow** (Aquatic ogres) (*Stormwrack / MM*): Aquatic ambushers lurking in the drowned ruins.
- **Chuul** & **Tainted Minion** (*Heroes of Horror*): Aberrations corrupted by Regiarix (the black dragon) and the ambient taint of the swamp.

### The North Wall & Underdark Depths
*Sourced mainly from Lords of Madness and Underdark.*
- **Grell & Grell Patriarch** (*Lords of Madness*): Tentacled terrors featured in ancient mines (like Arc 01 La Miniera).
- **Chitine & Choldrith** (*Underdark*): Lolth-sworn spider-humanoid researchers often found in Fungi Towers (e.g., Sonjak's researchers).
- **Fleshwarped Creatures** (*Lords of Madness*): Grotesque, failed drow experiments dumped in the Neverlight Grove.
- **Myconid Sovereign** (*Underdark*): Denizens of the great fungi towers, wielding powerful spores.

### Eternal Forge & Planar Nodes
*Sourced mainly from Monster Manual and Manual of the Planes.*
- **Crystalline Creatures** & **Xorn** (*Underdark / SRD*): Exploring the elemental Earth Node and the Forest of Living Crystal.
- **Mithral Golem** & **Magma Ooze**: Mighty constructs and slimes bridging the Fire and Earth planar nodes.

### Astral & Transitive Planes
*Sourced from Expanded Psionics Handbook and Monster Manual.*
- **Githyanki Knights / Gish**: Astral raiders riding red dragon mounts. They might appear in the Faerûn skies or the Underdark specifically hunting down mind flayer enclaves (like Zalkatar's) or retrieving stolen silver swords.
- **Githzerai Monks**: ascetic psionic monks from Limbo who also hunt illithids, but often clash with their Githyanki cousins.
